## üí° Key Sales Outreach Principles

### 1. **Start with Relationships, Not Sales**
- Focus on understanding their needs
- Offer value (free pilot program)
- Build trust first
- Sales will follow

### 2. **Personalize Every Email**
- Mention specific school programs
- Reference their initiatives
- Show you did research
- Make it about them, not you

### 3. **Follow Up Strategically**
- First follow-up: 7 days
- Second follow-up: 14 days
- Third follow-up: 30 days (if still interested)
- Don't be pushy, but be persistent

### 4. **Make It Easy to Say Yes**
- Free pilot program (no cost)
- Flexible timeline (3-6 months)
- Self-service onboarding (no training required)
- Ongoing support included

### 5. **Focus on Value, Not Features**
- Don't lead with "coding through basketball"
- Lead with "engaging STEM learning that students love"
- Focus on outcomes: engagement, learning, ease of use
- Address their pain points: student engagement, STEM interest
