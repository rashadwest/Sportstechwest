## Accessibility & Inclusion Checklist

### Language & Readability
- [ ] Reading level appropriate for audience
- [ ] Avoids jargon or explains terms

### Multiple Entry Points (Reggio)
- [ ] Story, game, code, visual options provided when applicable
- [ ] Alternatives for non-visual learners described

### Visual Design
- [ ] Color contrast sufficient
- [ ] No color-only cues
- [ ] Clear typography and spacing

### Interaction
- [ ] Clear instructions and goals
- [ ] Small steps with feedback
- [ ] Retry paths without penalty

### Cultural Responsiveness
- [ ] Inclusive examples and names
- [ ] Avoids stereotypes
