## Recording Specifications

### Recommended Format:
- **Type:** Video (screen recording with narration, or video with visuals)
- **Duration:** 15-25 minutes per episode
  - Story: 10-15 minutes
  - Skill Pit-Stop: 3-5 minutes
  - Wrap-up: 2-3 minutes
- **Format:** MP4, 1080p minimum
- **Audio:** Clear narration, background music optional

### Alternative Formats:
- **Audio-Only:** MP3, high quality (faster to produce)
- **Hybrid:** Video with embedded slides/illustrations
- **Screen Recording:** Story text with narration

### What to Include:
- Story narration (all 3 acts)
- Skill Pit-Stop explanation
- Visual elements (illustrations, diagrams)
- Clear transitions between sections
