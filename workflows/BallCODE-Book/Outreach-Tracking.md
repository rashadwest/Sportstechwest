## Outreach Email Templates

### Initial Outreach Email

**Subject:** Free Pilot Program: BallCODE - STEM Learning Through Basketball

**Body:**
Dear [Contact Name],

I hope this email finds you well. I'm reaching out because [School Name]'s commitment to [specific STEM initiative/program] caught my attention, and I believe you might be interested in a unique educational opportunity.

I'm the creator of BallCODE, an innovative program that teaches coding, math, and AI concepts through basketball. We're currently offering a **free pilot program** to select schools, and I'd love to explore if [School Name] would be a good fit.

**What BallCODE Offers:**
- Combines basketball excitement with hands-on STEM learning
- Teaches coding logic, math concepts, and AI fundamentals through sports
- Designed for elementary to middle school students (grades 3-8)
- Includes interactive game exercises and story-based learning

**What the Pilot Program Includes:**
- Free access to Episode 1 content and exercises
- Teacher training and support materials
- Student access to interactive game components
- Ongoing support throughout the pilot period (3-6 months)
- Opportunity to be featured in a case study

**What We're Looking For:**
- Feedback on content effectiveness
- Testimonials from educators and students
- 3-6 month commitment to pilot the program
- Willingness to share results (anonymized if preferred)

I'd love to schedule a brief 15-minute call to discuss how BallCODE could benefit your students. Would you be available for a quick conversation this week or next?

Thank you for your time and consideration.

Best regards,  
Rashad West  
Creator, BallCODE
