## Beta Testing Instructions

### For Educators

Thank you for participating in the BallCODE beta testing program! Your feedback is invaluable in helping us create the best possible educational content.

### What We're Testing

1. **Content Quality**
   - Is the story engaging for grades 3-8?
   - Is the educational content clear and effective?
   - Are the exercises appropriate for the age group?

2. **Educational Effectiveness**
   - Do students understand the state concept?
   - Are the learning objectives met?
   - Is the connection between basketball and coding clear?

3. **Usability**
   - Is the teacher guide helpful?
   - Are the exercises easy to implement?
   - Is the content ready for classroom use?

4. **Overall Experience**
   - Would you use this in your classroom?
   - What would you change?
   - What works well?

### Testing Process

1. **Review Materials** (30 minutes)
   - Read Episode 1 story
   - Review Skill Pit-Stop content
   - Review exercises and teacher guide

2. **Test with Students** (Optional but Recommended)
   - Use the teacher guide to teach Episode 1
   - Have students complete exercises
   - Observe student engagement and understanding

3. **Complete Feedback Form** (15-20 minutes)
   - Use the feedback form below
   - Be specific and honest
   - Include both positive feedback and areas for improvement

### Timeline

- **Week 1:** Review materials and test with students (if possible)
- **Week 2:** Complete feedback form and return
