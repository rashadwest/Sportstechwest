Style: Clean, minimal, futuristic, nighttime, starry sky, subtle holographic elements, purposeful design, not overloaded, family-friendly, dynamic composition, depth with foreground (children/court) and background (starry sky), soft glowing elements, cinematic nighttime lighting, 16:9 aspect ratio, website header format, professional quality, 4K resolution, clean aesthetic.

## üìê TECHNICAL SPECIFICATIONS

### Dimensions & Format
- **Aspect Ratio:** 16:9 (standard website header)
- **Recommended Resolution:** 3840 x 2160px (4K) or 1920 x 1080px (Full HD)
- **Format:** PNG (with transparency option) or JPG
- **File Size:** Optimized for web (< 500KB if possible)

### Color Palette (Exact Hex Codes)
- **Night Sky:** `#0A0E27` or `#0D1B2A` (deep dark blue)
- **Court Surface:** `#000000` (black)
- **Neon Blue Primary:** `#00D4FF` (bright cyan-blue for court lines and holograms)
- **Neon Blue Secondary:** `#0099FF` (electric blue for accents)
- **Orange Accent:** `#FF6B35` (vibrant orange for holographic elements)
- **White Stars:** `#FFFFFF` (pure white for stars and highlights)

## üéØ KEY ELEMENTS BREAKDOWN

### Foreground Elements (Bottom/Middle Layer)
1. **Basketball Court:**
   - Dark court surface (black)
   - Subtle glowing neon blue lines (boundaries, key, free-throw lines)
   - Two basketball hoops with soft glowing blue outlines
   - Court should appear naturally lit by neon accents and starlight

2. **Children Playing:**
   - 4-5 children, ages 8-12, diverse representation
   - Wearing blue t-shirts and shorts
   - Some dribbling basketballs (glowing blue)
   - Active basketball poses (dribbling, shooting, running)
   - Positioned across the court naturally, not crowded

### Midground Elements (Middle Layer)
1. **Purposeful Holographic Elements (MINIMAL):**
   - One or two subtle holographic displays floating above the court
   - Simple geometric patterns or data visualizations
   - Neon blue and orange colors only
   - Clean, purposeful design - not cluttered

2. **Basketball Trajectory:**
   - Single glowing basketball trajectory line or movement path
   - Neon blue color
   - Shows motion and flow

### Background Elements (Top Layer)
1. **Starry Night Sky:**
   - Deep dark blue night sky
   - Visible twinkling stars (white)
   - Clear, beautiful nighttime atmosphere
   - No clouds or obstructions

2. **NO TEXT ELEMENTS:**
   - Completely text-free image
   - No labels, no words, no buttons
   - Focus purely on visual elements
