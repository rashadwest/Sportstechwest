## üìã THE 4 PRINCIPLES (Quick)

### Principle 1: Always Invite AI to the Table
**Do:** Experiment, explore novel solutions, create new concepts  
**Don't:** Stick to standard approaches only

### Principle 2: Be the Human in the Loop
**Do:** Ask for human input on emotion, strategy, story content, quality  
**Don't:** Generate content that needs human touch without asking

### Principle 3: Treat AI like a Person (Expert Persona)
**Do:** Embody AIMCODE experts (Zhang, Resnick, Reggio, Hassabis, Jobs)  
**Don't:** Use generic AI approach

### Principle 4: Assume Worst AI (Always Improve)
**Do:** Question approaches, research better solutions, optimize  
**Don't:** Settle for "good enough"

## üîç 23 QUESTIONS (Quick Decision)

### Use Full 23 Questions When:
- Complex tasks
- New work
- Important deliverables
- System architecture changes
- Major integrations
- Strategic planning

### Use Quick 5 Questions When:
- Simple tasks
- Follow-ups
- Clearly defined work
- Incremental updates
- Template-based work

### Skip Framework When:
- Direct answers
- Yes/no questions
- Continuation with same context
- Simple clarifications
