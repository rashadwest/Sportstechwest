## When to Use Quick Version

✅ **Use Quick 5 for:**
- Simple, straightforward tasks
- Follow-ups to previous work
- Quick clarifications
- Tasks you've done many times before
- Tasks that are clearly defined

❌ **Use Full 23 Questions for:**
- Complex tasks requiring multiple steps
- New work or projects
- Important deliverables
- Tasks with unclear requirements
- Work that needs to match existing style/format
