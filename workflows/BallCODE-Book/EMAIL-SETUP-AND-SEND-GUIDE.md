1. Go to: https://workspace.google.com
2. Click "Get Started"

1. Google will ask you to verify you own ballcode.co
2. Add TXT record to your DNS (Google will provide exact record)
3. Wait for verification (usually 5-30 minutes)

1. Google will provide MX records to add
2. Go to your DNS provider (wherever ballcode.co DNS is managed)
3. Add MX records (usually 5 records provided by Google)
4. Wait for propagation (can take up to 48 hours, usually 1-2 hours)

#### Step 4: Create Email Address
1. In Google Workspace admin: Users ‚Üí Add User
2. Create: `rashad@ballcode.co` (or your preferred address)
3. Set password
4. Email is ready!

#### Step 5: Access Email

- **Gmail App:** Use your new email address
- **Other Clients:** Use IMAP/POP settings provided by Google
