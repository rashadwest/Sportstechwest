## Pilot Program Overview

**Purpose:** To pilot BallCODE's story-driven coding education program and provide feedback for improvement.

**What is BallCODE:**
BallCODE is a story-driven program that teaches coding, math, and AI concepts through basketball. Students read engaging stories, then practice with interactive games.
