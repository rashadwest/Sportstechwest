# Unified Prompting System
## The Complete Framework for Better AI Results

**Combines:**
- **Greg Brockman's o1 Framework** (Goals, Format, Guardrails, Context)
- **CLEAR Framework** (Clarity, Logic, Examples, Adaptation, Results)
- **Alpha Evolve** (Systematic Deep Learning)

---

## ğŸ¯ THE UNIFIED FRAMEWORK

### **1. GOAL (Brockman) + CLARITY (CLEAR)**
**What do you want? Be specific.**

**Questions to Ask Yourself:**
- What exactly am I trying to achieve?
- What does success look like?
- What are the constraints or limitations?
- Who is the audience?
- What format is needed?

### **2. FORMAT (Brockman) + LOGIC (CLEAR)**
**Define the structure you want back.**

**Questions to Ask Yourself:**
- What structure do I need? (List, table, script, outline, code, document?)
- What's the logical flow or sequence?
- How should this be organized?
- What steps should be followed?
- How does this build on previous work?

### **3. GUARDRAILS (Brockman) + ADAPTATION (CLEAR)**
**Set rules for accuracy and tone.**

**Questions to Ask Yourself:**
- What accuracy standards are required?
- What tone/style should be used?
- What should be avoided?
- What constraints exist?
- What flexibility is needed?
- What might go wrong?

### **4. CONTEXT (Brockman) + EXAMPLES (CLEAR) + ALPHA EVOLVE**
**Provide background, goals, audience, and build systematically.**

**Questions to Ask Yourself:**
- What background information is relevant?
- What are my goals and objectives?
- Who is the audience?
- What examples illustrate what I want?
- What similar work exists to reference?
- What foundational concepts need to be established first? (Layer 1)
- How should this build systematically? (Layers 2, 3, 4...)
- What systems thinking is needed?

### **5. RESULTS (CLEAR)**
**Define measurable outcomes.**

**Questions to Ask Yourself:**
- What does "done" look like?
- How will I know it's successful?
- What are the success criteria?
- What metrics matter?

- New work or projects
- Important deliverables
- Tasks with unclear requirements
