## Template: Career Connections Section

Copy this template for each episode:

```markdown
## Career Connections
*[Integrated with IBM SkillsBuild Teacher Toolkit]*

### Career Spotlight: [CONCEPT] in Tech Careers

[2-3 sentence explanation of how this concept is used in careers]

#### Careers That Use [CONCEPT]

1. **Career Name**
   - **What they do:** [1 sentence]
   - **How they use [concept]:** [1 sentence]
   - **Real example:** [1 concrete example]
   - **Education:** [Brief requirements]

2. **Career Name**
   - [Same format]

3. **Career Name**
   - [Same format]

[Include 3-5 careers total]

#### Career Connection Activity (15-20 minutes)

**Objective:** Connect Episode [X]'s [concept] to real-world tech careers.

**Materials:**
- Career research worksheet (optional)
- Internet access or career information handouts
- Presentation tools

**Instructions:**

1. **Career Selection (3 minutes)**
   - Students choose one career from the list
   - Each student/group gets a different career

2. **Research (8-10 minutes)**
   - Research: "How does [career] use [concept]?"
   - Find one specific real-world example
   - Identify 2-3 skills needed
   - Note education/training requirements

3. **Share Findings (5-7 minutes)**
   - Each student/group presents (1-2 minutes)
   - Class discusses similarities
   - Teacher highlights connections

**Discussion Questions:**
- [Question 1 related to concept and careers]
- [Question 2 about real-world applications]
- [Question 3 about student interests]

**Extension for Advanced Students:**
- [Optional deeper activity]

**Assessment:**
- Participation in research and presentation
- Quality of real-world example
- Connection made between concept and career
```

## Time-Saving Tips

### 1. Batch Similar Tasks
- Write all career spotlights in one session
- Create all extension activities together
- Review all episodes at once

### 2. Reuse Content
- Use similar activity structures across episodes
- Adapt discussion questions from Episode 1
- Reuse career research format

### 3. Focus on Quality, Not Perfection
- Get content complete first
- Polish in Day 6 review
- Don't overthink individual careers

### 4. Use Templates
- Career spotlight template (provided above)
- Activity template (provided above)
- Portfolio template (create once, reuse)

### 5. Prioritize Completeness
- Better to have all 12 episodes done than 3 perfect ones
- Can refine later
- Focus on demonstrating integration
