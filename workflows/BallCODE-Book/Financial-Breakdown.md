## Risk Assessment

### High Risk Factors
- Market acceptance of sports-coding hybrid
- Competition from established coding education books
- Changes in educational curriculum standards

### Mitigation Strategies
- Strong marketing to basketball/sports communities
- Partnerships with educational institutions
- Pilot programs with select schools
- Free sample chapters/content

## Recommendations

### Phase 1: MVP Approach (Reduce Costs)
- Start with digital-only format: **Save $4,625**

- Focus on core content first: **Save $10,000**
- **Revised Total: $68,175**

### Phase 2: Pre-Launch Validation
- Create sample chapters and gauge interest
- Run beta program with educators
- Collect testimonials and case studies
- Refine pricing based on feedback

### Phase 3: Staged Launch
- Digital launch first (lower cost, faster to market)
- Print launch after digital validation
- Educational market after initial success
