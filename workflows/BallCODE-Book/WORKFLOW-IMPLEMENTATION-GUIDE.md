## üöÄ How to Use This System

### Daily Morning Routine (5 minutes)

1. **Open `DAILY-WORKFLOW-TEMPLATE.md`**
2. **Fill out the PRE-WORK CHECKLIST section**
   - Answer all 10 rule categories
   - Identify your ONE domino for the day
   - Schedule your Deep Work window
   - Plan your context blocks
3. **Save the file with today's date** (e.g., `DAILY-WORKFLOW-2025-01-15.md`)

### Before Each Cursor Chat Session

1. **Open `CURSOR-CHAT-START-TEMPLATE.md`**
2. **Copy the entire template**
3. **Paste it into your new Cursor chat**
4. **Answer the quick status check questions** (2 minutes)
5. **State your session intent**
6. **Then proceed with your work request**

### End of Day (5 minutes)

1. **Open your daily workflow file**
2. **Fill out the END OF DAY METRICS section**
3. **Complete the reflection notes**
4. **Preview tomorrow's work**

## üö® Common Pitfalls to Avoid

### ‚ùå Skipping the Pre-Work
- **Problem:** Jumping into work without answering questions
- **Solution:** Make it a non-negotiable habit. No work until questions answered.

### ‚ùå Not Identifying ONE Domino
- **Problem:** Working on multiple priorities simultaneously
- **Solution:** Force yourself to choose ONE. Everything else can wait.

### ‚ùå Ignoring Energy Levels
- **Problem:** Working when energy is low, wasting time
- **Solution:** Match work complexity to energy level. Rest when needed.

### ‚ùå Not Tracking Metrics
- **Problem:** Can't improve what you don't measure
- **Solution:** Spend 2 minutes at end of day filling metrics.

### ‚ùå Skipping Weekly Audits
- **Problem:** Systems degrade over time
- **Solution:** Schedule audit time. Treat it like a meeting.
