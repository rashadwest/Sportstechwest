### Yearly Goals (2026)

**What are your 3-5 major goals for this entire year?**

**Goal #1: 10 Schools by CES (January 2027)**
- **Target:** 10 schools signed up and using BallCODE by the time CES is over (January 2027)
- **Why This Matters:** Establishes market validation, creates case studies, enables revenue generation, builds foundation for scaling
- **How to Measure:** Number of schools with signed agreements, active users, engagement metrics

**Goal #2: Full Team & Delegation System (Garvis + Thanos)**
- **Target:** Full team hired and trained, delegation system operational through Garvis and Thanos, you delegate effectively to team and AI systems
- **Why This Matters:** Enables you to focus on high-value work, builds scalable business, moves toward 36-month vision of care-free lifestyle
- **How to Measure:** Team members hired, delegation workflows operational, percentage of tasks delegated, your time freed up

**Goal #3: Fully Automated Build System (Garvis)**
- **Target:** Fully automated system for building products with only prompting, no manual intervention needed, zero issues
- **Why This Matters:** Eliminates manual work, enables rapid iteration, frees you for strategic work, creates competitive advantage
- **How to Measure:** Percentage of builds automated, prompt-to-deployment time, error rate (target: 0%), manual interventions needed

**Goal #4: Product Known & Loved**
- **Target:** BallCODE is recognized as a beloved product in the market, strong brand recognition, positive reviews, engaged community
- **Why This Matters:** Creates organic growth, enables premium pricing, builds sustainable business, fulfills vision of youth loving to learn through sport
- **How to Measure:** Brand awareness metrics, user reviews/ratings, community engagement, NPS score, social media mentions

**Goal #5: Community of Learners**
- **Target:** Active, engaged community of learners using BallCODE, sharing experiences, learning together
- **Why This Matters:** Creates network effects, organic growth, user retention, fulfills vision of building a learning ecosystem
- **How to Measure:** Community size, active members, engagement rate, user-generated content, retention rate
