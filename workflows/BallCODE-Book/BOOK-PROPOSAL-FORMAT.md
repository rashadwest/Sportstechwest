## QUALITY CHECKLIST

### Story Quality
- [ ] Story starts with basketball action (not concept explanation)
- [ ] Basketball terminology used naturally
- [ ] AI/math/coding concepts emerge FROM basketball needs
- [ ] 3-act structure followed
- [ ] Basketball success demonstrates concept mastery
- [ ] Engaging and adventurous (70% story, 30% skill)
- [ ] Word count within target (1,200-1,800 words)

### Educational Quality
- [ ] Learning objectives clearly met
- [ ] Age-appropriate language
- [ ] Concepts explained through basketball context
- [ ] Connection to game modes is clear
- [ ] Rigorous enough for advanced students

### Character Quality
- [ ] Characters are consistent with Character Bible
- [ ] Character arcs are clear
- [ ] Dialogue is age-appropriate
- [ ] Characters drive the story forward
