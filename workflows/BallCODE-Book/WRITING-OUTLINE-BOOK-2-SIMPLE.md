## PLOT (3 Acts - Broken into ~13 pages)

### ACT I: The Predictable Path (~400-500 words)
**What happens:**
- One-on-one situation, defender in front
- Nova tries to go same direction every time (always left)
- Defender reads her moves, blocks every path
- Arc detects: "Decision logic missing. No conditional response."
- Coach Circuit: "Read the defense. If they go left, you go right. That's the logic."
- Nova realizes: "Maybe I need to read and react?"

**End Act I:** Nova decides to learn decision-making

---

### ACT II: The Decision Tree (~400-500 words)
**What happens:**
- Defender gets better at reading moves
- Nova practices crossover dribble
- Learns: "If defense goes left, then crossover right"
- Arc: "If defender moves left, then crossover right. If defender moves right, then crossover left."
- Math: Probability - "If defender goes left 70%, crossover right 70%"
- Coding: IF/THEN logic - "IF defender goes left, THEN crossover right"
- Nova resists: "I just want to go straight, why decide?"
- Coach: "Basketball is about reading and reacting. Code is the same."
- Nova realizes: "This is like if/then code!"

**End Act II:** Nova understands conditional logic

---

### ACT III: The Smart Crossover (~400-500 words)
**What happens:**
- Final seconds, need to get past defender
- Nova reads defender's movement
- Uses if/then logic: "IF defender goes left, THEN crossover right"
- Success! Gets past defender, creates scoring opportunity
- Nova: "Conditional logic works! I can make smart decisions!"
- Connection: "Coding uses if/then to make decisions, just like basketball"

**End Act III:** Nova succeeds using conditional logic
