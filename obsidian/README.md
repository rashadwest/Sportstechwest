# Obsidian notes — Time vs Work

Vault-ready notes. Copy (or symlink) this `obsidian/` folder into your vault, or move the two subfolders into your existing structure.

## The split

- **`Time/`** — what you do with your time: personal-brand building, the SportsTechWest YouTube channel, training content. Judged by streaks and compounding, not invoices.
- **`Work/`** — what you do for work: BallCODE, client automation, anything with a customer or a deadline attached. Judged by deliverables and revenue.

The point of the split: a missed YouTube week should never hide inside a busy work week, and work deadlines should never silently eat the publishing streak. Two dashboards, two scoreboards.

If you consider the STW channel part of work instead, just move `Time/` notes into `Work/` — the structure is the point, not the filing choice.
