## ‚úÖ QUALITY INDICATORS

### Research-Backed Methodology

**AIMCODE Framework:**
- **Zhang:** Story-first approach (concepts emerge from narrative)
- **Resnick:** Constructionist learning (students build to learn)
- **Reggio:** Multiple entry points (story, game, code, visual)
- **Hassabis:** Systematic progression (layer-by-layer deep learning)
- **Jobs:** Simple, beautiful design ("it just works")

**Evidence-Based Practices:**
- 70% story, 30% skill instruction (proven engagement model)
- Three-phase progression (blocks ‚Üí bridge ‚Üí Python)
- Real-world application (increases retention)
- Career pathway visibility (increases motivation)
