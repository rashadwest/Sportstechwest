## AIMCODE Quick Start (10 minutes)

1) Pick Complexity
- Simple (Lightweight): 3-line CLEAR, BML micro, no research
- Medium (Hybrid): brief CLEAR, BML + 1 reference
- Complex (Full): full CLEAR, Alpha Evolve layers, research + expert lenses

2) CLEAR (max 5 lines total for Simple)
- C: one sentence objective and done criteria
- L: 2–3 steps
- E: 1 example/link
- A: 1 risk/constraint
- R: 1 measurable result

3) BML Micro
- Build: artifact(s)
- Measure: 1–2 metrics (process or product)
- Learn: 1 next action if metric misses

4) Story-First (when applicable) – check all 4
- Action first
- 3-act structure
- Concepts emerge from basketball needs
- Success proves learning

5) PR Template
- Paste CLEAR + BML, tick checks, add Story-First Score (0–5)
