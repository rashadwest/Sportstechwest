### 1. TUTORIAL MODE - "Learning with a Robot Teacher"

**What it is:**
Imagine you have a friendly robot teacher who shows you how to play basketball. The robot teacher:

### 2. CODING MODE - "Building a Basketball Recipe"

**What it is:**
This is like writing a recipe for making cookies, but instead of ingredients, you're using basketball moves. You pick different "ingredients" (dribble moves) and put them in order to create a play.

**What you do:**
1. You see a list of dribble moves on the left (like a menu):
   - POUND (bounce the ball hard)
   - CROSS (move ball from one hand to the other)
   - IN + OUT (fake one way, go the other)
   - BETWEEN THE LEGS
   - BEHIND THE BACK
   - HALF SPIN
   - SPIN

2. You drag these moves into a sequence (like making a sandwich):
   - START
   - POUND (3 times)
   - CROSS
   - LAY UP (shoot the ball)

3. You see your robot on the court
4. You click "PLAY" and watch your robot do exactly what you told it to do!

**Why it's cool:**
- You're the boss - you decide what happens
- You can try different combinations
- You can see your code turn into real movement
- You can rewind and try again if it doesn't work

**What you're learning:**
- How to give step-by-step instructions
- How to put things in order (sequences)
- How to think logically
- How to fix mistakes (debugging)

**The "Syntax" panel shows:**
- `Pound(F)` = Pound Forward
- `Cross(FR)` = Cross Forward Right
- `Bucket()` = Shoot the ball

This is like seeing the "secret code" behind your instructions!

### 5. CHESS MODE - "Strategic Basketball Chess"

**What it is:**
This is like chess, but with basketball! You have to think ahead and plan your moves strategically. You control both the player (offense) and the defender (defense).

**What you do:**
1. You see the court with a grid (like a chess board)
2. You place your PLAYER robot (green) where you want to start
3. You place your DEFENDER robot (grey) where you want to defend
4. You pick defensive moves:
   - DEFENSIVE RUN (chase the player)
   - DEFENSIVE SLIDE (slide to block)
   - DIAGONAL SLIDE (move diagonally to cover)
   - STANCE (get ready to defend)
   - HANDS UP (block the shot)

5. You plan your strategy: "If the player goes left, I'll slide right"
6. You execute your plan and see if it works!

**Why it's cool:**
- You think like a coach
- You plan ahead (like chess)
- You control both offense and defense
- You learn strategy and positioning

**What you're learning:**
- Strategic thinking
- Planning ahead
- Understanding offense and defense
- Spatial reasoning (where things are on the court)
