## What changed

STW News is not a one-off clip anymore. We are running it like a weekly show: **four Shorts per week** (Monday, Tuesday, Thursday, Friday), with Sunday reserved for picking the lineup and locking scripts.

## Why community apps are the headline

When a platform already has a massive athlete community—**Strava** is the obvious example—stories land differently. People already search those names. That is why **Strava acquiring Runna** (personalized running plans from 5K to marathon) is exactly the kind of item we package: two recognizable brands, one clear headline, and a straight line to “what this means if you train.”

## What runs under the hood

Daily scouting pulls high-signal feeds (press rooms, sports business, and tech coverage), scores candidates, and stacks a shortlist. On Sunday we write the queue for the week ahead, generate voice once per story, then ship video and metadata on schedule. The goal is not perfection on day one—it is **consistent shipping** so the algorithm gets enough at-bats to learn what your audience actually watches.

## What to watch next

If you care about sportstech as a builder or an athlete, follow the channel for the next four-pack. If you want the operational detail, the repo now carries the scout config, weekly selector, and dispatcher notes alongside the usual STW News packages.
