## üìû DISCOVERY CALL SCRIPT

### Pre-Call Preparation (5 minutes)
- [ ] Review school's website and programs
- [ ] Review contact's LinkedIn/profile (if available)
- [ ] Prepare 3-5 specific questions
- [ ] Have proposal document ready to share
- [ ] Test video/phone connection

### Opening (2 minutes)
"Hi [NAME], thanks for taking the time to talk with me today. I know you're busy, so I'll keep this brief - about 15 minutes.

As I mentioned in my email, I've created BallCODE - a program that teaches coding, math, and AI through basketball. I'm offering a free pilot program, and I'd love to see if it might be a good fit for [SCHOOL NAME]."

### Discovery Questions (10 minutes)
1. "What STEM or coding programs do you currently offer at [SCHOOL NAME]?"
   - Listen for: Current programs, challenges, student engagement

2. "What challenges do you face in engaging students with STEM subjects?"
   - Listen for: Engagement issues, resource constraints, curriculum alignment

3. "How do you measure success in your educational programs?"
   - Listen for: Metrics, outcomes, what matters to them

4. "What would make a pilot program valuable for your school?"
   - Listen for: What they need, what would be helpful

5. "What timeline works for your school year?"
   - Listen for: When they could start, semester preferences

### Present Solution (3 minutes)
"Based on what you've shared, here's how BallCODE could help:

- [Mention specific benefit based on their answers]
- Free pilot program - no cost to you
- Complete Episode 1 content ready to use
- Self-service onboarding - no training required
- Ongoing support throughout the pilot
- Opportunity to be featured in a case study

What we're looking for is feedback and a 3-6 month commitment to pilot the program."

### Close (2 minutes)
"If this sounds like it could be valuable, I'd love to send you the proposal document and Episode 1 content to review. Would that work for you?

[If yes] Great! I'll send that over today. When would be a good time to follow up - maybe next week?

[If no] I completely understand. Is there anything else I can help with, or would you like me to check back in a few months?"

### Post-Call Actions
- [ ] Send thank you email within 24 hours
