## Long (LinkedIn article, newsletter, bio)

**Option A — Complete overview**
```
I help entrepreneurs get started and connect to funding. It was never planned—it was instinctual. When asked to support founders, I just started helping. The biggest thing is getting started.

Over the years I've helped individuals start their own businesses, build operational systems, and connect with funding and investors. As a VC scout, I connect founders to capital—but that's one of many ways I help, not the core of what I do.

What I didn't expect: helping them has helped me learn. Every founder has sharpened my thinking on systems, capital, and what it really takes to go from idea to ownership. I've learned the VC game by being in it. Teaching is the greatest teacher.

Still Trucking Inc. is a perfect example—turning drivers into owner-operators through a structured course. Sportstechwest, BallCODE, BTE Analytics—different industries, same operating system: identify talent, build infrastructure, create access, scale opportunity.
```

I help entrepreneurs get started and connect to funding. Who this is for: individuals ready to take ownership of their future; organizations creating pathways for their people; founders and operators looking to leverage capital, technology, and systems.

What I do: help people start businesses, build operational systems, connect to funding. Sportstechwest (athletes), BallCODE (talent + tech), BTE Analytics (performance), Still Trucking Inc. (drivers → owners). Same system across industries. Teaching is the greatest teacher.
