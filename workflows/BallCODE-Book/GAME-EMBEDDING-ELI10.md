## ðŸ”§ HOW IT WORKS (Simple Version)

**It's like putting a window in your website:**
- The game is on another website (like a friend's house)
- But you put a "window" (called an iframe) on your page
- People can see and play the game through that window
- They never have to leave your page!

**Technical:** It's like embedding a YouTube video, but it's your game!
