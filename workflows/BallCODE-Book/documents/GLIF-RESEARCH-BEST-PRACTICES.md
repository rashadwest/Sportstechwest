## üìã RECOMMENDED WORKFLOW FOR YOUR 3 VISUAL ASSETS

### **Step 1: Preparation** (5 minutes)
1. ‚úÖ Open `documents/visual-assets/episode1-visual-prompts.json`
2. ‚úÖ Copy all 3 prompts
3. ‚úÖ Have Glif.app open in browser

### **Step 2: Community Search** (5 minutes)
1. Search Glif community for:
   - "basketball court diagram"
   - "educational diagram"
   - "basketball character"
2. If you find good glifs, remix them
3. If not, proceed with your prompts

### **Step 3: Generation** (20-30 minutes)
1. **Court Map:** Generate with prompt 1 (5-10 min)
2. **Shadow Press Scouts:** Generate with prompt 2 (5-10 min)
3. **State Diagram:** Generate with prompt 3 (5-10 min)
4. Generate 2-3 variations of each
5. Select best version

### **Step 4: Download & Save** (5 minutes)
1. Download high-resolution versions
2. Save to: `BallCode/assets/images/`
3. Use correct filenames:
   - `episode1-court-map-v1.png`
   - `episode1-shadow-press-scouts-v1.png`
   - `episode1-state-diagram-v1.png`

### **Step 5: Automation** (1 minute)
1. Run: `python3 scripts/add-visuals-to-book1.py`
2. Script automatically adds images to website
3. Done! ‚úÖ

**Total Time:** 35-45 minutes (vs 2-3 hours manual)
