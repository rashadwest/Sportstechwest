🎯 Key Finding
Corner 3s with hockey assists shoot 41.2% vs. 32.1% without. The extra pass creates superior shot quality through enhanced ball movement and defensive manipulation.
⚡ Coaching Implication
Players should be trained to make the "extra pass" even when they have a decent look. The hockey assist creates a +9.1% shooting advantage on corner 3s.

Ball Movement Strategy to Generate Corner 3s
🎪 Inside-Out Ball Movement
Start with post entry or drive to collapse defense, then kick out to wing, followed by swing pass to corner. This 3-pass sequence generates 73% of successful corner 3 attempts.
73% success rate with 3+ passes
🌪️ Weak Side Overload
Create 2-on-1 or 3-on-2 situations on strong side, forcing help defense to commit. Quick ball reversal to weak side corner creates open looks with 45% shooting percentage.
45% FG% on weak side corners
⚡ Transition Corner 3s
Early offense corner 3s (first 8 seconds) shoot 42% due to defensive scrambling. Players should immediately sprint to corners in transition.
42% in transition vs 38% half-court
🎯 Screen-the-Screener Actions
Use multiple screens to free up corner shooters. Screen-the-screener actions to corners generate 1.34 points per possession, highest of any play type.
1.34 PPP with STS to corner
🔄 Skip Pass Recognition
Train players to recognize help defense patterns. When help comes from weak side, immediate skip pass to corner creates 0.6 second advantage over closeout.
0.6s advantage on skip passes
