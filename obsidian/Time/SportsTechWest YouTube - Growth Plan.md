# SportsTechWest YouTube — Growth Plan (condensed)

Full version: `docs/YOUTUBE-GROWTH-PLAN.md` in the Sportstechwest repo.

## Three pillars

| Pillar | Format | Cadence | Job |
|--------|--------|---------|-----|
| STW News | 60–75s Shorts | 4x/wk (Mon/Tue/Thu/Fri) | Reach via searchable brand names |
| STW Breakdowns | 4–8 min long-form | 1x/wk (Wed) | Converts viewers → subscribers |
| IQ ambient | 1-hr audio/video | Library, not schedule | Search + session time |

## Non-negotiables

1. Everything ships **public**. Unlisted = invisible to the algorithm.
2. **Title formula:** `[Brand] + [verb] + [stake]` — brand names first, "STW News" stays in the opener graphic, not the title.
3. First 3 seconds = the headline, on screen and in voice.
4. End every Short with a question (comments are the cheapest ranking signal).
5. The streak is the strategy: finished weeks > perfect edits.

## Breakdown launch backlog (already written as blog posts)

1. NBA Draft Front Office Equation + simulator walkthrough ← **do first, draft season still warm**
2. Corner 3 as a decision tree
3. 10 tips youth basketball development (evergreen)
4. Championship basketball is systematic thinking
5. Measurement ≠ Understanding (2 parts)

Each Breakdown → 1–2 Shorts cut from it + embed back into the blog post + LinkedIn.

## 90-day targets

- 12 consecutive finished weeks (4 Shorts + 1 Breakdown)
- Long-form CTR ≥ 4%, avg view duration ≥ 40%
- +100–300 subscribers
- 1 outlier video (>10x channel median) → make 3 more like it
